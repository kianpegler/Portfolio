About Me

A little more about who I am, what I do, and how I got here.

I’m a Social Media & Marketing Manager based in West Yorkshire, with a love for live music, good books, baking, travel and the occasional theme park day.

Before moving into marketing, I spent two years as Head Tailor at Levi’s York — a role that taught me precision, customer care, and how to work quickly under pressure.

I later graduated from the University of York with a BSc in Interactive Media, where I focused on UX, UI and accessible design. My work explored how digital systems shape user experience, and how thoughtful design can improve everyday interactions.

Since 2025, I’ve been leading social media and marketing at Frankster’s, shaping the brand’s voice, campaigns and customer engagement across multiple platforms.

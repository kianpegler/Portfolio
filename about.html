About Me

A little more insight into me and what I do!

I’m a Social Media & Marketing Manager based in West Yorkshire, with a love for live music, good books, baking, travel and design.

Before moving into marketing, I spent two years as Head Tailor at Levi’s York in a role that taught me precision, customer care, and how to work quickly under pressure.

I later graduated from the University of York with a BSc in Interactive Media, where I focused on UX, UI and Marketing Design. My work explored how digital systems shape user experience, and how thoughtful design can improve everyday interactions.

Since 2025, I’ve been Creative lead as Social Media and Marketing Manager at Frankster’s, shaping the brand’s voice, campaigns and customer engagement across multiple platforms, hosting events, launching products and campaigns and initiating a total rebrand of our packaging, menus and more!.

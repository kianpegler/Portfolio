Projects

A selection of the work I’ve led across social media, marketing, design and digital content.

Frankster’s (2025–Present)

At Frankster’s, I’ve helped reshape the brand’s digital presence by modernising its tone, improving customer engagement and producing campaigns that resonate with local audiences. My work includes influencer events, community engagement, competitions, filming, photography and full campaign execution.

I also lead product launches, create content packs, manage communications and apply customer insights to improve campaign performance.

First Bus (2024–2025)

I supported the national X Team by managing real-time updates, web content, social publishing and community management. I worked closely with operational teams to resolve customer issues and maintain a consistent, trustworthy digital presence.

Design Work

As part of my work with Frankster’s, I’ve redesigned menus, screens, print media and digital assets to create a more modern, family‑friendly aesthetic. This includes branding updates, promotional materials and in‑store visuals.

University Projects

During my BSc in Interactive Media, I focused on UX, UI and accessible design. My dissertation explored improving workforce systems and user interfaces. I also completed speculative design briefs, including a clothing recycling app concept encouraging sustainable fashion habits.

Personal Projects

Outside of work, I enjoy experimenting with creative projects — from film edits and design concepts to small digital builds and playful side ideas. These projects help me stay curious, improve my skills and explore new creative directions.
